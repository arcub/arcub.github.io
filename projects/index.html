High-Altitude Reusable Rockoon (HARR) Rockoon (rocket attached to a balloon) was developed in 1949. Recently only few companies are using that technique as it has few disadvantages - balloon cannot be steered after launch, hard to direct rocket and control it, hence large area needed for landing. HARR project is aiming to solve that problems and make sending payload to stratosphere (and maybe to an orbit in future) easier, cheaper and affordable.
Rocket will be attached to a stratospheric balloon (or few) and send to stratosphere to take air samples and various measurements which we want to stream to ours computers. Also, need to be developed steering mechanism that allow rocket and balloon with launchpad come back to place where it started from.		 5
